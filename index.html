About Me

I'm an Android Developer and Scrum Master with nearly a decade of experience building mobile and full-stack applications. Currently at Critical TechWorks (a BMW Group company), I work on BMW's next-generation Infotainment system (IDC) built on Android Automotive.

My career blends deep technical expertise in Android development with strong leadership skills. I've set up teams from scratch, mentored 7+ junior developers, managed complex application ramp-ups and ramp-downs, and even ran two Scrum teams simultaneously. A side project I built — "Ambient Background Light" — won an internal BMW prize and led to the creation of an entirely new team.

Before Critical TechWorks, I worked at startups where I shipped everything from configurable Android apps and chatbot frameworks to full-stack SaaS platforms. I was part of Catxy, the world's first AR social network, featured at Web Summit PITCH 2016 among the "200 Most Promising Projects."
